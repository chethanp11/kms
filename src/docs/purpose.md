# KMS Purpose

KMS exists to transform immutable raw source material into finalized markdown knowledge that can be trusted by people and AI systems.

It is a governed knowledge control plane, not a chat interface, not a generic search engine, and not a raw document dump.

## Who it serves
- Knowledge Managers who maintain, review, and finalize knowledge
- Knowledge Consumers who browse and read finalized knowledge
- Downstream AI systems that need stable, governed markdown context

## What it preserves
- institutional memory
- business definitions and process rules
- source trace and review history
- finalized knowledge in `/wiki`

## What it separates
- maintenance from consumption
- source material from finalized truth
- governed publication from read-only navigation
